Music

Dream Diary

Representational records of dreaming in the form of genre-agnostic soundtracks (albeit tended towards interests in avant-prog / hypnagogic pop / post-industrial / onkyo), incorporating interest in the likes of early digital synthesis and sampling, rompler technologies, turn of the century digital multimedia, and broadly absurdist / dadaist / surrealist concerns relative to "outsider art" from an autistic perspective.

Material Resource

Application of dub, industrial, musique concrete, and radiophonic methodology to idiomatic concerns pertaining to folk (avant-folk, free folk, neofolk) / otherwise traditional music in a free improvisational context. Acoustic and digital instrumentation are combined for an uncanny aspect related to genre terminology such as (dark / ritual / tribal) ambient / drone / post-industrial, and so on.
