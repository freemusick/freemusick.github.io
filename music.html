Music

Dream Diary

Representational records of dreaming incorporating avant-prog, hypnagogic pop, post-industrial, onkyo, and other artistic influences such as absurdism, dadaism, and surrealism. Informed by interests in early digital sampling, turn-of-the-century digital multimedia, and notions of outsider art considered from an autistic perspective.

Material Resource

An exploration of folk and traditional music through the application of dub, industrial, musique concrete, and radiophonic techniques. Combining acoustic and digital instrumentation in a free improvisational context, encompassing elements of dark ambient, drone, and post-industrial.
